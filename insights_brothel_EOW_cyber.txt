Cases related to brothels:

-- The brothel related cases increased from 20 in the last year to 31 in this year, 2025.

-- 9 brothel related cases were registered in September, 2025.

-- 132 people were rescued from brothels this year, whereas only 51 were rescued in the previous year.

-- 109 people were arrested this year, whereas only 22 were arrested in the previous year. 

-- The above points indicate that either the number of brothel cases that were detected increased this year and also appropriate actions were taken more efficiently this year, as compared to the previous year, 
OR
Most of the cases went undetected in the previous year
OR
The number of brothel cases were truly lesser in the previous year and they increased this year.

-- No. of arrests per case in the previous year were 1.1, which means 1 person per case on average. It was 3.516 this year, which means 3-4 people per case on average.

-- No. of people rescued per case were 4.25 this year which means 4 people on average, whereas it was 2.55 last year, 2-3 people on an average.
